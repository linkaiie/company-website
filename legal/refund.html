Refund Policy

This policy explains how refunds work for prepaid credits and subscriptions.

Last updated: 2025-12-12

Scope

This Refund Policy applies to purchases of subscriptions and prepaid credits for LinkAI Route.

Prepaid credits

  • Unused credits: refundable within 30 days of purchase.
  • Used credits: non-refundable once consumed through API/workflow usage.
  • Partial refunds: if you request a refund, we may refund the unused portion only.

Subscriptions

  • You can cancel at any time to stop future renewals.
  • Subscription fees are generally non-refundable for the current billing period, except where required by law.
  • After cancellation, you typically retain access until the end of the paid period.

Charge disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact us first so we can help resolve it quickly (disputes/chargebacks may delay account access while investigated).

How to request a refund

Email our billing desk with your account email and invoice identifiers:

billing[at]linkai.ie

Refunds (when approved) are issued to the original payment method and may take several business days to appear, depending on your bank.

Need billing support?

Contact us with invoice details and we’ll help resolve it quickly.
