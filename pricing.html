Frequently asked questions

Do credits expire?

Credits stay active as long as your account is active. We send reminders when balances go unused for 90 days.

Can we top up automatically?

Yes. Set thresholds per project or workspace and LinkAI Route will charge the card or invoice on file.

What happens when we switch models?

No additional fees. The unified API lets you change providers without new contracts or SDKs.

Need help convincing finance?

We provide invoice templates, reconciliation exports and can talk directly with your finance lead.
